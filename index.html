I am a PhD student working under the supervision of Prof. M. V. Joshi at Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT), Gandhinagar, India. As a part of my PhD thesis, I have been working on techniques for inpainting, simultaneous inpainting and super-resolution, and auto-inpainting for digital repair of damaged facial regions in statues and cracked regions in heritage scenes.

I have submitted my PhD thesis titled "Novel Techniques for Auto-inpainting in Heritage Reconstruction" in September 2016 and awaiting reviews. Presently, I am looking for research opportunities in the fields of computer vision, image processing and machine learning.

At DA-IICT, I have also worked as a junior research fellow (JRF) on the Indian Digital Heritage (IDH)-Hampi project. I completed M.Tech. in Computer Engineering under the supervision of Prof. M. A. Zaveri at S. V. National Institute of Technology, Surat, India in 2010 and B.E. in Information Technology from Finolex Academy of Management and Technology, Ratnagiri (The University of Mumbai) in 2008.
